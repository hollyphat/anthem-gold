  • I don't fully understand your business model with the exchanges. Do you mean that you receive a % of the trading fees?

    Our business model is simple. Initially we make the majority of our profits from additional margin of issuing and selling 1,000 AGLD (each AGLD is backed by 1 gram of gold) and the cost of a 1 kilogram gold bar. Our estimated AGLD margins are included on our Business Model slide (Estimate of 15% Gross Margin in Year 1).

    Over time, we earn revenue from additional channels including issuing additional asset-backed and utility-based tokens, breakage revenue from storage fee margins after 36 months of included storage and wallet-related revenues such as from card swipe and loan origination fees. Additional information is available in our 5-year proforma financial projections.

  • It says you will "initially use the Ethereum blockchain"? Can you explain what that means? Are there plans to not use Ethereum's blockchain long term?

    AGLD is a fully gold-backed Ethereum token built on the ERC20 Ethereum Token Standard. At the end of 30 months, a new AGLD token is to be issued on the most time-efficient, cost-efficient and relatively secure blockchain as exists at the time of issuance. For first six months after the new AGLD token launch, we will make all 1st edition AGLD redeemable 1-to-1 with the new 2nd edition token and no new 1st edition AGLD tokens will be issued. We will work with exchanges to enable clients to easily swap to the newest AGLD in their wallets who wish to do so. Starting after the end of the 39th month, .09% of the gold backing the token will be sold every 3 months to cover insured storage costs.
